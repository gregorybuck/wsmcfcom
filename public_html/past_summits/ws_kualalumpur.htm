WSMC2014: Children's media makers urged to be bold and creative
Media professionals of the 21st century have been urged to be bold, to act differently, imaginative and creative on behalf of children and young people. 

12 Sep 2014
Summit calls for universal charter on media content for children
Experts in children's media from around the world, meeting in Malaysia, have issued a Declaration calling for a universal charter of guiding principles for making content for children.
12 Sep 2014
Romania Radio National Orchestra gala concert a highlight of WSMC
Delegates to the 2014 World Summit on Media for Children in Malaysia were treated to special gala concerts by the world-renowned Romanian Radio National Orchestra.
