3rd WORLD SUMMIT- GREECE 2001

The Third World Summit on Media for Children was organised by the European Children’s Television Centre (ECTC) under the supervision Hellenic Audiovisual Institute
The Summit attracted over 850 delegates from 80 countries. There were four main themes to the Summit, Going Global, Media for All, New Technologies and Children Have a Say. To find more details of this Summit, explore its own website.

Greece 2001

A Final Report has been prepared, presenting the essence of the Summit and a general view of the outcomes of the representatives. Additional material from the Summit archive will be added online during 2003.
